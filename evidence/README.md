# Evidence Folder

This folder mirrors the real workflow structure.

Real evidence files belong here during local use, but they are ignored by Git. Only templates and guide files are committed.

Recommended local structure:

```text
evidence/
  work/
    INDEX.md
    EVIDENCE_ROUTER.md
    EVIDENCE_CHEATSHEET.md
    evidence_manifest.json
    E01_example_feature.md
    E02_example_system.md
  projects/
    INDEX.md
    P01_project_name.md
    P02_project_name.md
```

Commit templates. Do not commit populated real evidence.

## What Counts As Evidence

Evidence should be specific enough to support resume bullets and interview follow-ups:

- what you built or improved
- the context and scope
- the stack involved
- the result or user/business impact
- what you personally owned
- what claims the evidence does not support

The goal is not to store every detail. The goal is to make truthful tailoring fast and defensible.
