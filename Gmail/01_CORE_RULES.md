# Outreach Core Rules

- Do not send email without explicit user confirmation.
- Do not create or attach a PDF unless the user explicitly asks.
- Do not invent recruiter details or prior relationship.
- Keep outreach short, specific, and human.
- Use the role angle from the tailoring workflow.
- Mention only evidence the resume can defend.
- Avoid dense keyword lists.
- Avoid overly polished or generic AI phrasing.

## Good Outreach Should Include

- the specific role or team
- one concise reason the fit is real
- one or two strongest matching signals
- a simple ask or closing line

## Avoid

- exaggerated claims
- vague passion statements
- long autobiography
- fake familiarity
- unrelated project details
