# Outreach Template

Subject:

```text
<Role> at <Company>
```

Body:

```text
Hi <Name>,

I saw the <Role> opening at <Company> and wanted to reach out. My background lines up most strongly with <specific role angle>, especially <evidence point 1> and <evidence point 2>.

I applied / am planning to apply and would appreciate being considered for the role.

Best,
<Candidate Name>
```

Use this as a structure, not as fixed wording.
