# Gmail / Outreach Rules Index

This folder mirrors the outreach side of the workflow.

Public files here are generic rules and templates. Real role briefs and recruiter details belong under `Gmail/role_briefs/` or a local email tool, and are ignored by Git.

Read order:

1. `Gmail/01_CORE_RULES.md`
2. `Gmail/02_TEMPLATE.md`
3. `Gmail/03_PHRASE_BANK.md` only when drafting final copy
4. matching role brief under `Gmail/role_briefs/` if present

Do not commit real recruiter names, emails, LinkedIn URLs, or private role notes.
